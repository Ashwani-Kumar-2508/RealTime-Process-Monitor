About OpsPulse

OpsPulse is a lab-ready monitoring suite that turns raw OS telemetry into clear, actionable insight.

Why it exists

Built for OS lab projects and real-world ops teams that need fast, reliable visibility into CPU, memory, disk, and process behavior without heavy tooling.

What you get

Real-time dashboards, live process control, clear status cards, and a clean UI that scales from classroom demos to production pilots.

How it works

FastAPI and WebSockets stream metrics every second; the UI renders charts and operational views instantly.

Core Principles

  • Clarity over noise: show only what matters.
  • Low latency: live updates without flicker.
  • Control with safety: terminate processes responsibly.

Roadmap

  • Alert persistence and incident history
  • Role-based access and audit logs
  • Exportable reports for lab evaluation
OpsPulse is designed to be extended for any lab or operational environment.
